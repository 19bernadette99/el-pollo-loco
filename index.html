El pollo loco

In the sun-scorched heart of the Fiery Jalapeño Desert, a lone hero named Pepe emerges from the sands with a mission hotter than the blazing sun: defend the desert from the Great Chicken Uprising.

Once peaceful, the desert has been overrun by a wild flock of rebellious chickens — from sneaky desert chicks to furious giant battle hens. These feathered foes have been corrupted by a mysterious enchilada energy buried deep beneath the sands. Now, they march under the command of the colossal chicken overlord, General Cluckzila, threatening to turn the desert into a pecked-apart wasteland.

But they didn't count on Pepe.

Armed with spicy Salsa Bottles, a fearless jump, and swift desert reflexes, Pepe leaps and sprints across rocky canyons and scorching dunes. Every thrown salsa bottle bursts like a fiesta bomb, sending chicken feathers flying.

Can Pepe out-dance, out-jump, and out-salsa the entire poultry army?
